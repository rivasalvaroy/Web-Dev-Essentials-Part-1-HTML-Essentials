Local School Wins Technology Grant

Grant Details

Jackson High School received a significant technology grant this month, promising to enhance learning with state-of-the-art computers and software. Over 500 students will benefit from this initiative.

Principal's Statement

The principal stated, "This grant is a game-changer for our school," highlighting the impact of the new resources.
